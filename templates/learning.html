Understanding Stock Valuation

Valuing stocks involves assessing a company's fundamentals to determine if the stock is overvalued or undervalued.

Fundamental Analysis

Fundamental analysis focuses on a company's financial health to assess its stock value. Key metrics include P/E ratio, EPS, and revenue growth.

Technical Analysis Basics

Technical analysis uses historical price data and chart patterns to forecast future stock price movements.
